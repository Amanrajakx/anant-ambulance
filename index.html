Common Questions

What is your response time?
Depending on your location, we usually arrive within 10–20 minutes in the city area. For outstation calls, we dispatch immediately and provide an ETA upon booking.
Do you provide interstate transfers?
Yes, we provide long-distance patient transfers across states with full ICU and ventilator support. Our team coordinates with destination hospitals in advance.
Is oxygen available in all ambulances?
Yes, every single one of our ambulances is equipped with oxygen cylinders and basic life support equipment. ICU ambulances carry advanced ventilator systems.
How do I pay for the service?
We accept cash, UPI, and card payments. For corporate clients, monthly invoicing is available. Payment is settled after the service is rendered — we never delay help for payment reasons.
Can I book in advance for planned hospital transfers?
Absolutely. You can book in advance via the form on this page, by WhatsApp, or by calling us. We confirm the booking and assign the appropriate vehicle based on medical needs.
What areas do you cover?
We are based in Mumbai and cover all of Greater Mumbai, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Pune, Nashik, and surrounding regions. We also handle pan-India long-distance transfers on request.
Are your Antim Sanskar services available for all communities?
Yes. We respectfully serve all communities and religions, and can coordinate with priests or religious coordinators as required by the family's tradition.
