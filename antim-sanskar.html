Questions

Families Often Ask

Do you provide services at night or on holidays?
Yes. Our Antim Sanskar team operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year — including all festivals and public holidays. Loss does not wait, and neither do we.
Can you help transport the body to another state?
Yes. We assist with embalming, documentation, and arrange transportation by road, rail, or cargo flight to any state in India. We handle all required permits.
What is the cost of the Antim Sanskar service?
Our Antim Sanskar packages start at ₹8,500 for Electric cremation and ₹10,000 for Traditional cremation, and vary based on services required, distance, and specific needs. We are fully transparent — no hidden charges.
Can you arrange for a priest or pandit?
Yes. We have a network of trusted priests and religious coordinators across communities who we can arrange on your behalf, based on your faith and customs.
How do I contact you at the time of need?
Call us on +91 90048 05097 or WhatsApp us on +91 98208 18626. Our coordinator will answer immediately and guide you through every next step with calm and care.
